Galactic Conquest homepage


Short Pitch

Galactic Conquest is a space exploration startup company that specializes in harvesting energy from the sun, using it to give our spaceships the energy necessary to travel faster than the speed of light.


Long Pitch

With many of the greatest scientists, astronomers, and engineers in the world, we are making progress by the minute to crack the code on intergalactic travel. We have set forth a plan to travel to the closest solar system to ours, Alpha Centauri. It is 4.37 light years away, but once we are able to travel at light-speed, we are extremely confident that it will open the gateway to figure out intergalactic travel at much faster paces.
